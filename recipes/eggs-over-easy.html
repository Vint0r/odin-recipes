Eggs Over Easy, Easily

Cooking eggs is easy. Why am I doing this recipe? Well, I found an extra easy way to cook your over easy eggs using a non-stick pan, it's lid, and some water!

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 Tablespoon of Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Non-Stick Pan With Lid
  • Tablespoon of Water

Steps:

  1. Set your stovetop burner to med-high heat
  2. Add butter
  3. Make sure the melted butter evenly coats the pan
  4. Add eggs
  5. After 15-30 seconds, add water, preferably next to eggs if any panspace is open
  6. Cover with lid
  7. Watch through lid if you have a glass lid. Once the top of the egg yolk turns translucently white, they're done! It should only take a minute or two
  8. Plate and salt and pepper to taste
